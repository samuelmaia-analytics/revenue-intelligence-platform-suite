# Case Study 02 - Discount Leakage Revenue Recovery

## Context

Discount policies increased sales volume but reduced realized net revenue in key categories.

## Business Problem

Teams lacked a structured view of where discount leakage was concentrated and how to act.

## Decision

Build a leakage analytics workflow with category-level prioritization and executive reporting.

## Execution

- Standardized raw sales ingestion and metric contracts.
- Produced category leakage ranking and scenario-based recovery estimates.
- Published executive summary in analytics dashboards.

## Outcome (Initial Baseline)

- Leakage concentration surfaced by category and channel.
- Quantified value opportunity to support budget and campaign decisions.
- Repeatable process for tracking recovery progress.

## Risks and Mitigation

- Risk: over-correction can hurt conversion.
- Mitigation: apply control groups and phased rollout by segment.
